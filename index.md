# Privacy Policy
**Last updated: March 14, 2026**

Surah Tawbah Quiz ("the App") is an educational tool developed by Quran Tech. This App is designed to provide users with a quiz experience based on religious lectures. 

Our guiding principle is "Privacy by Design": we believe that your data belongs to you.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not require you to create an account or provide an email address to use the App.

All user-generated data, including:
* Quiz progress and Mastery levels
* Bookmarks and personal notes
* Mistake history and quiz scores

...is stored **locally on your device only**. This data is never uploaded to our servers, shared with third parties, or used for advertising.

## 2. Permissions
To function correctly, the App may request the following permissions:
* **Internet Access:** Used solely for loading specific fonts or external educational links. No browsing history or device identifiers are collected.
* **Storage Access:** Used solely for the "Share Certificate" feature. This allows the App to temporarily save a certificate image to your device so you can share it.

## 3. Children’s Privacy (COPPA/GDPR-K)
These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. Because all data remains on your local device and is never transmitted to us, we have no way of accessing, storing, or sharing any data belonging to children.

## 4. Third-Party Services
The App does not use any third-party analytics (like Google Analytics), advertising networks (like AdMob), or data-sharing SDKs. 

## 5. Data Deletion and User Rights
Under regulations such as GDPR (EU) and CCPA (USA), you have the right to access or delete your data. Since we do not store your data on our servers, you can exercise these rights instantly by:
* Using the **"Reset All Progress"** button in the App Settings.
* Clearing the **App Cache/Data** in your Android/iOS System Settings.
* **Uninstalling** the App (this permanently deletes all local data).

## 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
